hmm it's really hard to tell but as you might already know, です is just a です and けど stands for "but", so feels like what you are not sure about here is なん, right?
なん here is not 何(:what) as in 何だそれ？(nanda sore: what is it?), it consists of two words; な(its original form is だ "the assertive auxiliary verb")＋ん(phonetically changed の, which changes the contents before it into a noun)
so supersuperliterally, そういうことなんですけど consists of そういうことだ(the way things are going now)＋の(that)＋です(it is)＋けど(but)→it is the way things are going now, but why?（そういうことなんですけど、なんでですの？）
anyway, you better think of なんです as a more assertive way to say だ/です i guess
